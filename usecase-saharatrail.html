Project Overview

A desert gem that deserved to be found.

Sahara Trail is a Moroccan travel agency specialising in private desert tours, camel treks, and authentic Berber experiences across Merzouga, the Draa Valley, and beyond. With years of on-ground expertise and glowing word-of-mouth reviews, their product was world-class.

Their digital presence was not. A dated website, zero SEO strategy, and no paid acquisition meant they were losing high-intent international travellers to competitors every single day. ProBoost was brought in to change that entirely.

We rebuilt Sahara Trail's brand, website, and full digital acquisition engine from scratch — positioning them as the premium choice for discerning travellers seeking Morocco's most authentic desert journeys.

The Challenge

Real problems. Real stakes.

Sahara Trail came to us with a clear pain point: beautiful experiences, broken discovery. Before we touched a single pixel, we audited everything. Here's what we found:

  • No organic visibility — the site ranked for almost nothing. Competitors dominated every high-intent search term from "Morocco desert tours" to "Merzouga camel trek."
  • A website that repelled trust — slow load times, no SSL on sub-pages, poor mobile experience, and imagery that didn't reflect the premium they were delivering.
  • Zero conversion architecture — no clear call-to-action flow, no booking enquiry system, no social proof surfaced where it mattered.
  • No paid acquisition — they were entirely dependent on referrals and OTAs, giving away up to 25% commission on every booking.
  • Inconsistent brand voice — copy was written in three tones across the same page. No positioning. No story.

"We had the best tours in the region. But nobody could find us. ProBoost completely changed that — the phone started ringing within weeks of the new site going live."

— Owner, Sahara Trail Tours
