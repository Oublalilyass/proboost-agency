Instagram · Reels Strategy

The Riad Reel Formula —
30 Seconds to a Booking

WHY IT WORKS

Instagram Reels receive 22% more reach than standard posts and 5× more engagement than static images. For Marrakech tourism businesses, the platform's visual nature perfectly matches the city's extraordinary aesthetic — a single well-shot 30-second Reel of a riad courtyard at golden hour can generate 200+ direct booking inquiries organically.

  • Post 4–5 Reels per week minimum. The algorithm rewards consistency — frequency triggers the discovery flywheel. Batch-film 20 Reels in a single shoot session and schedule them across the month.
  • The 3-second hook rule. The first 3 seconds determine whether someone watches or scrolls. Start with the most visually striking shot — the water feature, the zellige ceiling, the rooftop sunset — before any title card or text.
  • Use trending audio strategically. Pair property visuals with currently trending audio tracks. Instagram's algorithm distributes content with trending audio to non-followers more aggressively. Check the "Trending" section in Reels before each post.
  • Keywords in captions and alt text. Instagram indexes caption text for search. Include "riad Marrakech", "Medina Marrakech", "rooftop Marrakech" naturally in every caption — this drives Instagram search discovery, separate from hashtag reach.
  • French and English captions simultaneously. Write bilingual captions — French first (largest market), English second. This doubles your content's reach with zero additional effort.
📊 Expected: 200–500% increase in profile reach within 90 days of consistent Reels posting
02
TikTok · Organic Discovery

TikTok for Marrakech Tourism —
Organic Reach No Other Platform Offers

WHY IT WORKS

TikTok's For You Page algorithm does not require a follower base to distribute content widely — a brand-new account with a great video can hit 100K views in 48 hours. In a city as visually extraordinary as Marrakech, this is the single most powerful organic marketing tool available in 2026, particularly for the 18–35 traveler segment.

  • POV content converts best. "POV: You wake up in your Marrakech riad" — first-person perspective content drives the strongest emotional response and save rate. Saves signal TikTok's algorithm to redistribute content further.
  • Participate in travel trends. "Travel red flags", "Things I wish I knew before visiting Marrakech", "Where locals actually eat in Marrakech" — trend-participation content reaches beyond your target audience to travelers researching all of Morocco.
  • Series content builds loyalty. A weekly series — "Inside the Marrakech Medina", "7-day Morocco road trip", "Traditional Moroccan cooking explained" — trains the algorithm and your audience to expect and seek your content.
  • Use Moroccan Arabic and Darija strategically. Snippets of Darija (Moroccan Arabic) in videos create authenticity that foreign-produced content can never replicate — a genuine competitive advantage for Marrakech-based businesses.
📊 Expected: 10K–100K organic views per strong video, zero ad spend required

04
UGC · Influencer Strategy

User-Generated Content —
Your Guests Are Your Best Marketing Team

WHY IT WORKS

User-generated content (UGC) — photos and videos created by your guests — consistently outperforms brand-created content in engagement, trust, and conversion rates. 79% of travelers say UGC highly influences their booking decisions. In Marrakech, where guests naturally want to photograph and share their experience, systematically encouraging and reposting UGC is one of the highest-ROI social strategies available.

  • Create an Instagram-worthy moment in your property. A designated photo spot — a beautiful doorway, a tiled fountain, a rooftop corner at golden hour — gives guests a reason to photograph and tag your account. Design it intentionally.
  • Include a UGC request card in every room. A beautifully designed card with your Instagram handle and a request to share: "Share your stay with #[YourRiadName] for a chance to be featured." Simple, effective, zero cost.
  • Micro-influencer partnerships over macro. A Morocco-focused travel blogger with 8,000 engaged followers drives more direct bookings than a lifestyle influencer with 200,000 unengaged ones. Target influencers whose audience matches your guest profile, not those with the largest numbers.
  • Always request permission before reposting. A DM requesting permission is not just good practice — it reopens a conversation with a past guest, reinforces the positive memory of their stay, and often generates a Google or TripAdvisor review as a byproduct.
📊 Expected: UGC accounts for 60–70% of the most high-converting social content for Marrakech tourism
05
WhatsApp Business · Direct Conversion

WhatsApp Business —
Turn Followers into Direct Bookings

WHY IT WORKS

WhatsApp has 98% open rates in Morocco. Every social media inquiry that routes through WhatsApp Business — rather than Booking.com's messaging system — is a conversation you own, a commission you save, and a guest relationship you build directly. This is the link between social media discovery and OTA-free revenue.

  • Add a WhatsApp booking link in your Instagram bio. Use wa.me/[yournumber] with a pre-filled message: "Bonjour, je voudrais réserver votre riad — pouvez-vous m'envoyer vos disponibilités?" This removes all friction from the inquiry process.
  • Set up automated welcome messages. A WhatsApp Business automated greeting that responds instantly (even at 2am when a Paris traveler is planning their trip) dramatically increases inquiry-to-booking conversion rates.
  • Create a WhatsApp catalog. List your room types, prices, availability windows, and key amenities in the WhatsApp Business catalog — guests can browse without waiting for a response, and you can share specific room links in conversations.
  • Post-stay WhatsApp follow-up. Send a brief WhatsApp message 48 hours after checkout: a thank-you and a direct link to your Google review page. This is the single most effective review generation tactic available in Morocco.
📊 Expected: 65–75% higher conversion rate on WhatsApp inquiries vs. email or OTA messaging

Paid Social · Facebook & Instagram Ads

Paid Social for Marrakech Tourism —
When and How to Run Ads That Work

WHY IT WORKS

Organic social builds a long-term asset. Paid social fills your calendar in the short term — particularly useful in low season, for new openings, or when targeting specific international markets (French, German, British travelers) with precision that organic reach cannot replicate.

  • Retargeting is where the ROI is. Facebook and Instagram Pixel retargeting — showing ads to people who have already visited your website but not booked — consistently outperforms cold audience acquisition. The cost-per-booking from retargeting is typically 5–10× lower than cold traffic campaigns.
  • Lookalike audiences from past guests. Upload a list of past guest emails to Meta's Ads Manager and create a lookalike audience of people with similar profiles. These audiences have dramatically higher booking intent than general interest targeting.
  • Target specifically for travel intent. Meta's travel-intent audiences (people who have recently searched for travel in Morocco, people whose Facebook behavior indicates imminent international travel) are available at low CPM and perform well for Marrakech accommodation.
  • Video ads outperform static ads 3:1 for Marrakech tourism. A 15-second property video — even filmed on an iPhone with basic editing — consistently outperforms professionally-photographed static image ads in click-through and booking conversion.
📊 Expected: 3–8× ROAS on retargeting campaigns for Marrakech accommodation

The Marrakech Content Hierarchy

Prioritize your content production in this order — highest booking-intent first:

  1. Property walk-through Reels (Instagram + TikTok) — 30–60 second virtual tours of your riad, restaurant, or spa. The single highest-converting content type. Film weekly, different time of day, different lighting conditions.
  2. Guest testimonial videos — a 20-second clip of a happy guest speaking directly to camera is worth 10 professionally produced brand videos in terms of trust and conversion.
  3. Experience content — "A morning in our riad kitchen making msemen", "Sunset from our rooftop over the Medina". Creates emotional desire rather than just awareness.
  4. Educational content — "How to find the best riads in Marrakech", "What to eat in the Medina". Positions your brand as the authority; gets shared by people planning Morocco trips.
  5. Behind-the-scenes content — your team preparing the riad, the kitchen before service, the hammam being set up. Builds authenticity and E-E-A-T signals that Google now uses as indirect ranking inputs.
What Not To Do

Social Media Mistakes That Are
Killing Your Visibility in Marrakech

These are the most common social media management mistakes made by tourism businesses in Marrakech — and the specific fix for each one.

📵

No Booking Link in Bio

Thousands of profile visits with no way to book — every visit is a lost conversion opportunity.

✓ Fix: Add wa.me booking link as primary bio link, updated weekly with current availability.
🌍

English-Only Content

France is Morocco's #1 inbound tourist market. English-only content misses 60%+ of your potential audience.

✓ Fix: Bilingual captions (FR first, then EN) on every post. Zero extra time after the first week.
📅

Inconsistent Posting

Posting 10 times in one week then disappearing for a month. The algorithm penalizes inactivity harder than it rewards bursts.

✓ Fix: Batch-create content monthly. Schedule 5 posts/week minimum with a tool like Later or Meta Business Suite.
🔇

Not Replying to Comments/DMs

Instagram and TikTok's algorithm measures response time. Accounts that reply within an hour receive dramatically more distribution.

✓ Fix: Set phone notifications for comments and DMs. Reply within 2 hours maximum during business hours.
📸

Poor Photography Quality

Marrakech is one of the world's most photogenic cities. Low-quality photos in a beautiful city signal low-quality service to potential guests.

✓ Fix: Invest once in a 2-hour professional shoot. 50 high-quality images covers 3 months of content at consistent posting pace.
📊

No Analytics Review

Posting without reviewing what performs means repeating what doesn't work indefinitely. Most accounts waste 60% of their content budget on formats that don't drive reach.

✓ Fix: Monthly 30-minute analytics review. Double down on the top 3 performing post formats. Cut the rest.
