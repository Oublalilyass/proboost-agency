How the Audit Works

Our scoring engine evaluates 6 key Local SEO signal categories used by Google Maps to determine your business's ranking position.

⭐
Review Strength

Volume, recency, and average rating of your Google reviews. The single most weighted ranking factor in local search — accounts for 30% of your score.

🔤
Keyword Optimization

Presence of high-intent keywords in your business name, description, categories, and posts. Worth 20% of your total visibility score.

📸
Photos & Content

Number of photos, quality signals, and visual completeness of your Google Business Profile. Listings with professional photos get 42% more direction requests.

📋
Profile Completeness

How complete your GBP is: hours, website, phone, categories, attributes, services and amenities. Missing fields directly reduce your ranking.

⚡
Activity & Engagement

Recent posts, review responses, Q&A activity — Google rewards actively managed profiles with higher visibility in competitive markets like Marrakech.

🌐
Website Authority

The SEO strength of your linked website and its NAP consistency with your Google Business Profile. No linked website = significant ranking penalty.

The Hidden Cost of Poor Local SEO in Marrakech

Marrakech receives over 3 million international visitors per year — and the vast majority begin their search for riads, hotels, restaurants, and experiences on Google Maps. A business ranking in position 1–3 of the local pack receives 70–80% of all clicks. Position 7 or below receives fewer than 5%.

For a boutique riad averaging €180 per booking, appearing on page 2 of Google Maps instead of the top 3 can mean losing 12–20 bookings per month — translating to €2,000–€4,500 in monthly lost revenue going directly to better-optimized competitors.

What Google Maps Actually Ranks On

Google's local ranking algorithm weighs three primary factors: Relevance (how well your profile matches the search query), Distance (proximity to the searcher or area specified), and Prominence (your overall authority including reviews, links, and engagement).

For tourism businesses in Marrakech, Prominence is the most improvable factor — heavily influenced by review volume, review recency, and profile completeness. A riad with 180 reviews and a 4.8 rating will consistently outrank one with 12 reviews and a 4.9 rating.

Local SEO in Morocco: The Opportunity

Morocco's tourism sector is growing rapidly — arrivals hit 14.5 million in 2024 and are projected to reach 17 million by 2026. Yet the majority of Marrakech hospitality businesses have severely under-optimized Google Business Profiles: incomplete descriptions, few photos, no posts, unanswered reviews.

A business that invests in Local SEO today can achieve top-3 Google Maps positioning within 60–90 days, even in competitive categories like "riad Marrakech" or "restaurant medina Marrakech."

  • Build a fast, mobile-optimized website with integrated direct booking
  • Rank for specific intent keywords: "riad Marrakech pool", "boutique hotel medina Marrakech"
  • Optimize Google Business Profile for local map searches
  • Collect guest emails and run post-stay review request campaigns
  • Offer a best-price guarantee that justifies booking direct
  • Use retargeting to recapture visitors who viewed but didn't book
