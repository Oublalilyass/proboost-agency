Local SEO · Google Business Profile SEO Local · Fiche Google

Optimize Your Google Business Profile

Optimisez Votre Fiche Google Business

Why it works: Travelers search locally now — "Riad in Medina Marrakech" = direct leads, not just traffic. Google Maps results appear above organic results on mobile, making this your highest-ROI SEO action.
Pourquoi ça marche : Les voyageurs cherchent localement — "Riad Médina Marrakech" = des leads directs. Les résultats Google Maps s'affichent au-dessus des résultats organiques sur mobile.
  • Claim your profile with category "Hotel" or "Bed & Breakfast"
  • Add 10+ high-quality photos (rooms, pool, terrace, courtyard, exterior)
  • Fill description with keywords: "luxury riad Marrakech Medina, pool, airport transfer"
  • Collect 5+ real reviews from past guests on Google + TripAdvisor
  • Post weekly updates: "New room available for summer 2026"
  • Réclamez votre fiche avec la catégorie "Hôtel" ou "Chambre d'hôtes"
  • Ajoutez 10+ photos de qualité (chambres, piscine, terrasse, patio, extérieur)
  • Rédigez une description avec mots-clés : "riad luxe Marrakech Médina, piscine, transfert aéroport"
  • Collectez 5+ vrais avis de vos clients sur Google + TripAdvisor
  • Publiez des mises à jour hebdomadaires : "Nouvelle chambre disponible été 2026"
Expected: 30–50 local leads/month within 3 months
Résultat attendu : 30–50 leads locaux/mois en 3 mois

Pourquoi ça marche : La plupart des riads utilisent du jargon professionnel. Les vrais voyageurs cherchent naturellement : "riad 3 nuits près Jemaa el-Fna". Les mots-clés longue traîne ont 3× plus de taux de conversion et 10× moins de concurrence.

Common Mistakes

Mistakes That Are Killing
Your Riad Bookings Right Now

🔍
Not Indexed by Google
Type site:yourriad.com in Google. If nothing shows up, Google doesn't know you exist — no matter how good your rooms are.
Fix: Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
📱
No Google Search Console
Without GSC you're flying blind. You don't know which keywords bring visitors, which pages have errors, or whether Google is even crawling your site.
Fix: Set up GSC + submit XML sitemap today
💨
High Bounce Rate
If visitors leave immediately, Google assumes your page didn't answer their question — and pushes you down. A fast, relevant, mobile-optimized page keeps visitors engaged.
Fix: Improve page speed + add clear booking CTA
📸
Bad (or No) Photos
Photos are the #1 conversion factor for riads. Low-resolution, poorly lit images lose bookings before a single word is read. Professional photography pays for itself in the first week.
Fix: Invest in professional HDR photography
🌐
English-Only Website
French is the dominant search language for Morocco tourism. An English-only riad website misses 60%+ of potential bookings from European travelers who search in French.
Fix: Add French + Arabic versions with hreflang
📊
No Booking Strategy
A website that "just exists" without a conversion funnel — no clear booking form, no WhatsApp button, no urgency triggers — is a brochure, not a sales tool.
Fix: Add booking widget + WhatsApp CTA + reviews
