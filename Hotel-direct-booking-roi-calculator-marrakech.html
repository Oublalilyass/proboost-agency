The complete checklist

8 steps to turn OTA guests into direct guests

This is the rollout order we use with hotels and riads in Marrakech — start at step one, and don't skip to remarketing before the booking engine actually works on a phone.

01

A booking engine that works in French, English and Arabic

Your website needs a real-time booking engine showing your own rates and availability — not just a contact form. If a guest from Casablanca, Paris or London can't book a room in under two minutes on their phone, they'll close the tab and open Booking.com instead.

Quick win
02

Price parity, minus the commission

Match your OTA rate exactly, or beat it slightly with a "book direct" perk — free airport pickup, a welcome tea, late checkout. Guests need a concrete reason to leave the platform they already trust.

Quick win
03

Google Hotel Ads and Google Business Profile

Most searches for "riad Marrakech" or "hotel near Jemaa el-Fnaa" start on Google Maps, not on an OTA. A verified Business Profile with photos, direct booking links and recent reviews puts you in the metasearch box before a guest ever sees a commission-hungry platform.

Core
04

Local SEO around what guests actually search

Guests booking a riad also search "best places to visit in Marrakech", "restaurants in Marrakech" and "spas in Marrakech" before they ever search your property name. Ranking for that intent, and linking those pages back to your booking engine, builds the trust and traffic an OTA listing can never give you.

Core
05

WhatsApp booking for last-minute and returning guests

A huge share of Marrakech bookings happen last-minute or by referral. A WhatsApp Business number on every page lets a guest confirm a room in a two-message exchange, with zero commission and zero third-party data ownership.

Quick win
06

A review-generation flow you own

OTA reviews build the OTA's trust, not yours. Ask every direct and OTA guest, at checkout, to leave a Google review — this compounds your local ranking and gives future direct guests the same social proof they'd find on Booking.com.

Core
07

Remarketing to past guests and site visitors

Install a pixel so past guests and abandoned bookings can be retargeted with a direct offer for their next Marrakech trip — riad guests have unusually high repeat-visit and referral rates once they've experienced the property in person.

Growth
08

Package direct-only experiences

Bundle a spa afternoon, a guided food tour, or a day trip to the Atlas Mountains with a direct booking only. This is unmatchable on an OTA listing and turns your website into a genuinely better offer than any third-party platform.

Why it matters here

What this means for tourism companies across Marrakech and Morocco

Marrakech's accommodation market is unusually fragmented — hundreds of independent riads, boutique hotels and guesthouses competing for the same search traffic as international chains. That fragmentation is exactly why a direct-booking strategy pays off faster here than in a market dominated by a handful of large hotel groups: independent properties that rank locally and convert on their own site can out-compete OTA listings on trust alone, without ever matching an OTA's marketing budget.

The same logic applies beyond hotels. Riads compete with the growing short-term rental market covered in our Airbnb ROI calculator for Morocco, and every property — hotel, riad or apartment — benefits from being the destination guests find when they search for what to do once they land: where to eat, where to relax, and what to see. That's the exact intent behind our guides to Marrakech's best places to visit in 2026, the city's top restaurants, and its best spas — content that captures a future guest weeks before they book, and points them straight to a direct reservation instead of an OTA search box.

Frequently asked

Direct booking ROI, answered

What is a hotel direct booking ROI calculator?
It's a tool that compares what you currently pay in OTA commission against the cost of running your own booking engine, local SEO and marketing — so you can see, in MAD, whether shifting bookings direct actually pays off for your specific property.
How much commission do OTAs really take in Marrakech?
Most riads and boutique hotels report commissions between 15% and 20% on Booking.com and up to 25% on Expedia, with rates often higher during Marrakech's peak season from October through April.
Is direct booking realistic for a small independent riad?
Yes — small properties often see faster payback than large hotels because a single well-optimized page, a WhatsApp number, and consistent Google reviews can meaningfully shift a modest number of monthly bookings, which is all the calculator above requires to turn a profit.
Should I drop OTAs entirely?
No — OTAs still bring first-time discovery, especially from international guests unfamiliar with Marrakech. The goal is to keep OTAs for new-guest acquisition while systematically capturing repeat guests, referrals and last-minute bookings direct.
How long until a direct booking strategy pays for itself?
Based on the inputs modeled above, most Marrakech properties see a payback period of six to nine months once the booking engine, local SEO and review flow are all running together — the calculator gives you a payback estimate for your exact numbers.
