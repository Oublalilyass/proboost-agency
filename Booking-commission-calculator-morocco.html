Everything You Need to Know About Booking.com Commission in Morocco

Tout ce que Vous Devez Savoir sur la Commission Booking.com au Maroc

How Booking.com's Commission Is Calculated

Booking.com calculates commission on the total booking value — meaning the full room rate multiplied by the number of nights, before any guest-paid taxes. This is a critical distinction: if your room rate is €200/night for a 3-night stay (€600 total), Booking.com takes €90 at the standard 15% rate. You receive €510. The commission is deducted directly from your payout, not added on top for the guest.

What many Moroccan riad and hotel owners don't realize is that the effective commission can be significantly higher than 15% when you factor in Genius discounts (where you bear the cost, not Booking) and the Preferred Partner surcharge. A hotel in the Genius program offering a 15% discount to Genius members effectively subsidizes that discount from its own revenue — pushing the true cost of a booking to 17–22% of rack rate.

"A typical Marrakech riad on Booking.com with 20 bookings per month at €300 average is paying more than €10,800 per year in commission — enough to fully fund a professional website, SEO campaign, and direct booking system with significant ROI remaining."

The Genius Program: What It Actually Costs You

Booking.com's Genius program positions itself as a benefit — better placement in exchange for offering discounts to Genius members. The reality for Moroccan properties is more nuanced. The discount (10–25%) is absorbed entirely by your revenue, not by Booking.com. They still take their full commission on the discounted rate. So your net per Genius booking is lower on two fronts: lower room rate AND full commission on that lower rate.

  • Genius Level 1: 10% discount to Genius members · effective commission 16.5%+
  • Genius Level 2: 15% discount to Genius members · effective commission 17.75%+
  • Genius Level 3: 20% discount to Genius members · effective commission 19%+
  • In all cases, Booking.com benefits from more bookings — you bear the cost
