The Challenge

A rooftop about to open.
No one knew it existed.

MO'MA is a brand-new rooftop dining concept in the heart of Marrakech — a city already saturated with competing restaurant noise on social media. The team had an exceptional product, a stunning space, and a refined identity — but zero digital presence and weeks to launch.

They needed to create anticipation, establish credibility, and convert interest into opening-night reservations — all before a single table was ever served.

02

Our Answer

A complete digital opening — built in weeks.

ProBoost designed and executed a full digital launch strategy centred on storytelling: the mystery of a "Coming Soon", the reveal, and the opening moment. We combined brand identity rollout, social media content, a landing website, and a curated launch campaign to turn MO'MA's first post into a destination people were already waiting for.

The result was a rooftop that felt established from day one.
