Frequently Asked Questions — ProBoost Agency Morocco

What types of businesses does ProBoost Agency work with in Morocco?

ProBoost Agency specialises in digital solutions for Morocco's tourism sector including travel agencies, tour operators, Airbnb hosts, riad owners, restaurants, cafés, spas, hammams and boutique hotels — primarily in Marrakech but serving all major Moroccan cities.

Does ProBoost Agency offer SEO services in Marrakech?

Yes. ProBoost Agency offers full local and international SEO services targeted at travelers searching for experiences in Morocco. We optimise for English and French search queries and implement Google Business Profile strategies.

Can ProBoost build a direct booking website for my riad or Airbnb in Marrakech?

Absolutely. We design and develop direct booking websites for riads, Airbnb hosts and short-term rental properties across Morocco, integrated with payment solutions and booking calendars to reduce dependence on third-party platforms.

How long does it take to build a website with ProBoost Agency?

Most tourism websites are delivered within 3 to 6 weeks depending on complexity. The process includes discovery, strategy, design, development, and launch — followed by ongoing support and SEO monitoring.
